From the Founder's Desk

With over a decade of experience in academic coaching and student mentorship, Mantu Sharma founded Ignite Institution with a singular vision: to make quality education accessible and transformative for every student in Kolkata. His hands-on approach, deep subject expertise, and commitment to each student's individual journey have made Ignite a trusted name in the region.
